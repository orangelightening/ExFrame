Domain Survey

                                    
Surveys all patterns within a single, existing domain. Choose this when you want comprehensive coverage of one topic area.

                                

                            

                            

                                üîç
                                

                                    
Neighbourhood Survey

                                    
Defines a filter that spans multiple domains. The system searches across all domains to find patterns matching your criteria. Perfect for cross-cutting topics like "quick recipes" or "data science patterns."

                                

                            

                            

                                üåå
                                

                                    
Universe Survey

                                    
Surveys all patterns across all domains in the universe. Use sparingly - this is comprehensive and resource-intensive.

                                

                            

                        


                        
                        

                            
                            
                        


                        
                        

                            
                            
                            

                                

                                    What this does: Your definition becomes a search query that scans across ALL domains to find matching patterns.
                                

                                

                                    Examples:

                                    ‚Ä¢ "quick desserts under 20 minutes" ‚Üí finds matching patterns from cooking, baking, dessert domains

                                    ‚Ä¢ "machine learning model evaluation" ‚Üí finds patterns from python, data_science, ML domains

                                    ‚Ä¢ "authentication and security best practices" ‚Üí spans webdev, security, backend domains
